2
10–30s (What is Anemia): "Anemia mein blood mein iron ya hemoglobin kam ho jaata hai. Iron hamare red blood cells banata hai — jo oxygen carry karte hain. Kam iron = thakaan, chakkar, saansi phoolna." Keep it conversational.
3
30–50s (Symptoms + Why Women Are More at Risk): "Symptoms: thakaan, pale skin, aankhon ka white part paheela, nakhoon kaale, concentration nahi. Periods ki wajah se iron loss hota hai — isliye women zyada vulnerable hoti hain."
4
50–70s (Diet Tips): "Iron-rich foods khao: paalak, chana, rajma, khajoor, anaar, eggs, chicken liver. Vitamin C ke saath khao (nimbu dalo) — absorption badh jaata hai. Doodh aur chai iron absorb hone nahi deti — khaane ke 1 ghante baad lo."
5
70–85s (Testing + CTA): "Ek simple CBC blood test se pata chalta hai. Agar symptoms hain — test zaroor karwao." Direct, actionable close.
Key Points Covered
What is anemia Why women are at risk Key symptoms Iron-rich Indian foods CBC blood test
Caption

Baby Milestone Chart — 0 to 12 Months
Child Development  ·  High Save + Share
▾
Slide-by-Slide Content
Slide 1 — Cover
"Aapka Baby Kya Seekh Raha Hai? 👶 0–12 Mahine Ka Milestone Guide" Colorful, playful design.
Slide 2 — 0–3 Months
Smiling (social smile at 6 weeks). Tracking faces with eyes. Lifting head briefly. Cooing sounds. Reacts to sounds.
Slide 3 — 3–6 Months
Head control strong. Rolls over (front to back). Reaches for objects. Laughs aloud! Babbling starts. Recognizes parents' faces.
Slide 4 — 6–9 Months
Sits without support. Transfers objects hand to hand. Says "mama/dada" (not specific). Stranger anxiety starts. Solid foods begin.
Slide 5 — 9–12 Months
Crawls or pulls to stand. Pincer grasp (picks small things). Waves bye-bye. Says 1–2 real words. Follows simple instructions.
Slide 6 — When to Consult
If baby isn't: smiling by 3 months, babbling by 6 months, sitting by 9 months, or says no words by 12 months — consult your pediatrician. Every baby is different, but milestones matter.
